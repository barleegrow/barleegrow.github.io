Design Principles

Barbara Stacey

White Space

Serious Eats

seriouseats.com

Serious Eats has plenty of white space and a clean design. It is easy to navigate to the recipe as well as to share it by tapping any of the icons on the top of the page. It is visually appealing and not just because the photos are beautiful.

Visual Heirarchy

AAR

aar.org

The Association of American Railroads has good visual hierarchy witht he logo on the top and the main focus on the breaking news story at the top. The black contrast draws your eye to the story and the white below then becomes a second focla point.

Hick's Law

AWPA

awpa.com

The American Wood Preservers Association makes it easy to choose from a limited menu. It is a good example of Hick's law, reducing the decision making time. With a more complex menu, the user can get frustrated and leave the site, or take a longer time to make a decision. Sometimes less is more.
