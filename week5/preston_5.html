Outdoor Fitness Can Make You Happier

I.B. Smiley


Studies show that exercise can make you happier. University of Vermont researchers have found that just 20 minutes of exercise can last 12 hours. Exercise has the greatest effect on our bad moods. It increases feel-good brain chemicals, reduces stress hormones, and has been shown to remedy both depression and anxiety.


Compared to indoor workouts, outdoor workouts makes us feel more energetic and less tense. The effects are multiplied when done in the natural environment rather than in an urban setting. Studies show that physical activity in natural areas gives your brain and body a double dose of feel-good chemicals and helps you cope with stress in more positive ways.


Check our weather forecast so that you can be aware of the best times and days to get out there and improve your fitness while you improve your mood!
