Idaho Weather


People everywhere say that if you don't like the weather, sitck around a few minutes and it will change. That can be as true in Southeastern Idaho as it is anywhere. The unique landscape and terrain features can create a day with sun in the morning, rain at noon, and snow overnight and all in the month of May!


Natives of the area for centuries have paid close attention to the weather patterns and have altered their activities accordingly. The Shoshoni and Bannock cultures were primarily nomadic and used the resources of the high desert to advantage by moving frequently to the best hunting and grazing areas. When the weather became cold, they migrated to warmer climates.


When white settlers cam in to the area and began farming, some of the natives were surprised that they would stay during the colder parts of the year.
