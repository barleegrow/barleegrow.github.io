Fish Haven:
A Tiny Town for Big Adventure


Fish Haven is an undiscovered paradise for sportsmen. Located along the shores of beautiful Bear Lake, this area is perfect for fishing, kayaking, water skiing, and other water sports. The scenery is breathtaking and the residents are friendly and accomodating.


If water sports aren't your favorite thing, there's golf, hiking, bird watching and even some caves in the area. You can enjoy time in the saddle of a bicycle or on horseback.


You may leave Fish Haven, but the memories you make here will last a lifetime.
