Site Purpose

The SportWeather site is specifically for people who enjoy outdoor sports. It is a place where instructors, coaches, individuals, and teams can go to find weather information specific to their training needs. SportWeather will offer:

  • How the weather will affect training
  • What the weather will be like on a given day
  • Optimal training times
  • Weather for specific activities
