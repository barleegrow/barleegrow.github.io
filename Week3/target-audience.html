Coach Schrader

The track coach at the local high school is interested in the weather for his training programs.
He checks Sportweather to see how the weather looks for running during the times that he has scheduled practice.
Coach wants to make sure that his athletes are not in danger while they train hard for their meets.
He asks the athletes to check the weather and dress accordingly.
They can then be prepared with extra water or sports drinks, layers of clothing to wear after they work out, or specific snacks.

Sherri Satterly

Sherri mainly uses SportWeather to check conditions on the slopes.
She wants to make sure that her students have the best possible experience so she checks to see ski conditions on the days she has classes scheduled.
That way she can be prepared for whatever weather is coming her way and can adjust her schedule as needed.
Sherri also makes sure that her classes know to check SportWeather to see how weather conditions are on the way to the resort.

Ridge Porter

Ridge is a mountain bike enthusiast who wants to go pro.
He checks SportWeather to see what the conditions are out on the trails.
He looks at the hourly weather to make sure that he doesn't run into anything that would keep him from getting the best times on his training rides.
Ridge makes sure that he has the proper gear for any adverse weather he may run into on his rides.
